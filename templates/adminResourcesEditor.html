Chippewa Valley Dance Club
www.dance.spinstep.com/Main/CVDC

The Chippewa Valley Dance Club (CVDC) is a non-profit ballroom dance club that will be sponsoring five Saturday night dances this year. Also, five Sunday afternoon dances will be included this season which feature music presented by Goggin Ballroom Dancing. Dances are held at the St Mary's Community Center, 1812 Lynn Av, Altoona, WI.

CVDC Membership Membership dues are $89 per person, which entitles you to attend all ten Saturday & Sunday dances. Nonmembers pay $16 per person at the door on Saturdays, Sunday dances are $10 per person.

Members enjoy:

  • A $41 discount compared to the non-member fees
  • ABetter bands, because your advance payment helps us book good bands in advance
  • A stronger dance club: when you join us, all of your membership dues go to the dance club.
